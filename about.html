Wiles Enterprises
Sound & Lighting Services

Wiles Enterprises is a Cambridge owned and operated business offering lighting and sound services to the community.

Specialising in theatre sound and lighting, we offer solutions for small to medium scale productions.

Get in touch with our friendly team to find out if we can meet your needs.
